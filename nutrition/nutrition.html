Nutrition for Fitness

Nutrition Before Exercise

Fuel your body with the right nutrients before a workout. Aim for a meal or snack that combines carbohydrates and protein about 30-60 minutes prior to exercising.

  • Banana with peanut butter
  • Greek yogurt with berries
  • Oatmeal with nuts

Nutrition During Workout

Stay hydrated and consider quick energy sources if your workout lasts over an hour. Here are some options:

  • Sports drinks
  • Energy gels
  • Fruit slices (like oranges or watermelon)

Nutrition After Exercise

Replenish your energy and aid recovery with a balanced meal that includes protein and carbs within two hours post-workout.

  • Grilled chicken with quinoa and vegetables
  • Protein smoothie with spinach and banana
  • Whole grain toast with avocado and eggs
